Young Mathematicians Workshop on Several Complex Variables 2016
November 10-13, 2016
Room 052 (Nov. 10) and Room 126 (Nov.11-13), Graduate School of Mathematical Sciences, The University of Tokyo, Japan

Speakers and Titles

  • Hiroki Fujino (Nagoya University): Quasisymmetric embedding of discrete subset and its extensibility
  • Hendrik Herrmann (University of Cologne): Embedding of CR manifolds with circle action
  • Genki Hosono (University of Tokyo): On convergence along geodesics between plurisubharmonic functions
  • Seungro Joo (GAIA, POSTECH): On the scaling methods by Pinchuk and Frankel
  • Yoshikazu Nagata (GAIA, POSTECH): On Lie group structure of automorphism groups
  • Ngoc Cuong Nguyen (GAIA, POSTECH): The Dirichlet problem for a complex Hessian equation on compact Hermitian manifolds with boundary
  • Ryosuke Nomura (University of Tokyo): Negative holomorphic sectional curvature and the Kähler-Ricci flow
  • Inyoung Park (GAIA, POSTECH): Joint Carleson measure of composition operators on the weighted Bergman spaces defined on polydisks
  • Feng Rong (Shanghai Jiao Tong University): Quasi-Reinhardt domains and Cartan's Linearity Theorem
  • Yuya Takeuchi (University of Tokyo): CR geometry of Sasaki-$\eta$-Einstein manifolds
  • Jujie Wu (Henan University): A global approximation result by Al Taylor and the strong openness conjecture in C^n
  • Sungmin Yoo (GAIA, POSTECH): Lower bound of holomorphic sectional curvatures of the Bergman metric
  • Chengchen Zhong (Capital Normal University): the holomorphic automorphism of the Cartan-ellipsoid type domain
